CHRONO

Agentic Longitudinal Blood Intelligence

Gemma 4 Good Hackathon 2026 · Health & Sciences Track

Google DeepMind Gemma 4 E4B + 26B On-device AI
World's first system that detects pre-cancerous metabolic states from routine blood test history — years before imaging can. Three validated Nobel-adjacent signals unified for the first time.
  • 3-tier agentic pipeline: Gemma 4 E4B vision OCR → biomarker normalisation → Gemma 4 26B ReAct agent performing longitudinal metabolic drift detection across 15+ biomarkers including HbA1c, LDH, CBC, and ferritin.
  • Achieved <200ms on-device inference latency on consumer hardware with zero PHI cloud transmission — making the architecture HIPAA-compliant by design, not by policy.
  • Trident Signal: three independently validated cancer signals — Warburg Index Velocity, Biological Age Velocity (validated on 308,156 UK Biobank participants), and Immune Collapse Velocity — co-tracked for the first time as a unified metabolic fingerprint.
  • GCP Confidential VM enclave powers the Protocol-99 agentic triage workflow — Gemma 4 26B reasons through five tools, eliminates confounders, generates a clinical dossier, and routes to an oncologist with a one-tap confirm interface.

INCIDENTMIND

IncidentMind

Autonomous Production Incident Resolution

Meta PyTorch OpenEnv Hackathon 2026 · Bangalore Finale

Meta Finalist Live Demo RL Research

73% P1 incident resolution within a 5-step horizon — 3.1× faster than median human L1 escalation latency. Shipped as a pip-installable PyPI package.

  • OpenEnv and Gymnasium-compliant RL environment simulating 9 production incident archetypes across 3 difficulty tiers — covering memory leaks, cascading failures, DNS misconfigurations, TLS certificate expiry, schema migration race conditions, and feature flag disasters.
  • Confidence-gated observation space that dynamically reveals environment state, forcing agents to learn cost-of-information tradeoffs — paired with a dense reward function penalising blast-radius multiplied by clarification-budget overspend.
  • Benchmark suite spanning 500+ rollout episodes across 4 baselines — PPO, DQN, A2C, and Random — with fully deterministic graders requiring no LLM judge, ensuring reproducible scores.

CASE_STUDY: OPTI-FAB

OPTI-FAB

IESA DeepTech Hackathon 2026 · Semiconductor Edge AI

63.9% latency reduction from 8.3ms to 3.0ms, measured on real RTX 4050 hardware — not simulated. Decisions made at 50–68% frame completion while the wafer is still under the scanner.

  • Stream-aware production inference pipeline for 300mm wafer inspection: MobileNetV2 backbone, TensorRT FP16 quantisation, and a confidence-gated early-exit layer — achieving 1,262 frames per second at 0.79ms inference latency, 3.34× faster than CPU baseline.
  • 90% classification accuracy across 8 defect classes with <1% accuracy degradation versus FP32, demonstrating precision-efficiency co-optimisation. Benchmarked on NVIDIA Jetson-class edge hardware for direct fab-floor deployment.
